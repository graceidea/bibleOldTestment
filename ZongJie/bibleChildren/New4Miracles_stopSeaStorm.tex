\section{平靜風和海——馬可福音 出埃及记 14:10,21-22,26-28/Mark 4:35-41}
\subsection{中文}
\begin{itemize}
\item 出埃及记 14:10,21-22 \\
法老临近的时候，以色列人举目看见埃及人赶来，就甚惧怕，向耶和华哀求。 摩西向海伸杖，耶和华便用大东风，使海水一夜退去，水便分开，海就成了干地。 22以色列人下海中走干地，水在他们的左右作了墙垣。
\item 出埃及记 14:26-28\\ 耶和华对摩西说：“你向海伸杖，叫水仍合在埃及人并他们的车辆、马兵身上。” 27摩西就向海伸杖，到了天一亮，海水仍旧复原。埃及人避水逃跑的时候，耶和华把他们推翻在海中， 28水就回流，淹没了车辆和马兵。那些跟着以色列人下海法老的全军，连一个也没有剩下。

\item 羅馬書 10:17 信道是从听道来的，听道是从基督的话来的。

\item 馬可福音 Mark 4:35-41 \\當那天晚上，耶穌對門徒說：「我們渡到那邊去吧。」 36 門徒離開眾人，耶穌仍在船上，他們就把他一同帶去；也有別的船和他同行。 37 忽然起了暴風，波浪打入船內，甚至船要滿了水。 38 耶穌在船尾上枕著枕頭睡覺。門徒叫醒了他，說：「夫子！我們喪命，你不顧嗎？」 39 耶穌醒了，斥責風，向海說：「住了吧！靜了吧！」風就止住，大大地平靜了。 40 耶穌對他們說：「為什麼膽怯？你們還沒有信心嗎？」 41 他們就大大地懼怕，彼此說：「這到底是誰？連風和海也聽從他了！」
\end{itemize}

\item Exodes 14:10,21-22\\

All that night the Lord drove back the sea with a strong east wind. And so he made the sea become dry ground. The water was split. And the Israelites went through the sea on dry land. A wall of water was on both sides.

Then the Lord told Moses, “Hold your hand over the sea. Then the water will come back over the Egyptians, their chariots and chariot drivers.” So Moses raised his hand over the sea. So all the king's army that had followed the Israelites into the sea was covered. Not one of them survived.

\item Romans 10:17 \\faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of God.

\item Mark 4:35-36\\
That evening, Jesus said to his followers, “Come with me across the lake.” 36 He and the followers left the people there. They went in the boat that Jesus was already sitting in. There were also other boats with them. 

\item Mark 4:37-38a\\ A very strong wind came up on the lake. The waves began coming over the sides and into the boat. It was almost full of water. 38 Jesus was at the back of the boat, sleeping with his head on a pillow.

\item Mark 4:40-41\\Jesus said to his followers, “Why are you afraid? Do you still have no faith?” The followers were very afraid and asked each other, “What kind of man is this? Even the wind and the waves obey him!”
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Extended Discussion}
\begin{itemize}
\item What do we learn from the order of Jesus after hearing his followers' request?
\item What might be the reasons that caused his followers to have no faith in Jesus' promise?
\begin{itemize}
\item They may did not pay attention to Jesus when Jesus said: “Come with me across the lake.” 
\item Lack of understanding of Jesus' word;
\item Immidiately forgot about Jesus' word after hearing;
\item The followers may have been afraid of the storm and the rough waters, and this fear may have overwhelmed their faith in Jesus.
\item The followers may have had previous failure experiences where they had faced similar situations, which could have affected their confidence in Jesus' promise.
\item It is also possible that the disciples' faith in Jesus was not yet fully developed, and they were still in the process of learning to trust him completely.
\end{itemize}
\item What do we learn from these followers when confronted with trouble?
\end{itemize}
